Interests

One of my most cherished endeavors was leading the charge at Cresskill For Police. In the summer of 2020, we embarked on a mission to support our local law enforcement. Through the ingenious creation of custom-designed shirts, emblazoned with the emblem of our dedicated police force, we wove together a successful fundraising initiative. The endeavor wasn't merely about numbers; it was about giving back, about standing shoulder to shoulder with those who selflessly serve our community. Every shirt sold was a thread in the tapestry of our support, and as the proceeds poured in, so did the spirit of generosity. We were able to present a donation of over $5,000 to C.O.P.S (Concerns of Police Survivors), a testament to our entrepreneurial zeal and unwavering commitment to making a difference.
